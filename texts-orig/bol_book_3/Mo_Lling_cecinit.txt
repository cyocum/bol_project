Rochuala la nech legas libru intí ances in mbidbaid iss é fessin as bidbu. Rochuala la cech nduine nod léga cech óen arric slabraid for ríg cin cech a ndena. Ro scribad a libru Dé ní tu ro marb acht is é tucad diles braith do chach dogoa bethaid vel brath. Moinach Casil comadas rí lasa marbtar drochdoene atá Mumu lais i ssíd rop maith Dia don dagríg Ro ansat na drochdoene oro marbtha a ceile. bendacht Crist for ríg roda croch ba moch canait a séire. Diambad rim contuased rí ropad ní a chland dia éis drochdaíne lais dochum bais ilar degdoene ma méis. Timmairg na doene trena airchis na doene truaga tol Meic Dé cech a ndena iss e do less rochuala. R.
