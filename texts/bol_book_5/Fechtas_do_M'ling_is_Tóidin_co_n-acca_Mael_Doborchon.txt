Fo  bíth is e rop anmchara do bennachaid do M'ling. For íarair ataam a chlerig or Mael Doborchon. For iarair Crist ón ar Mo Ling. Is mor in t-échtsin doronsat Iudaidi Crist do chrochad. Dobémais ar   cumang ica thessarggain or Mael Doborchon. Gaibthi Mo Ling iarum  i mmedón muine draigin. ⁊ dober a chocholl for cualle i mmedón in muine. Amal bad é Crist tra in sucut cinnas donesartha. Sreid Mail Doborchon a étach de ⁊ dosrála in muine secha cona dib lámaib co rroacht in cocholl co mbai eter a dí láim. Is     amlaidseo donesarcaindse ar Maíl Doborchon & ro bátar a sreba fola assa innaib. Amra sin a Mael Doborchon ar Mo Ling. Rot bia a lóg la Crist. ni theilcfet fir iffirn fuil essiut co bráth ⁊ noco tacerat demna for t'anmain. Airg a manaig út ar Mo Ling frisin certán  frisin n-iarair la Máel Doborchon. Gaibthi remib in certan co  foruim for di leiss  ana gabra áit i rraba. Conda ruc. Mael Doborchon   leis a eochu dochum a thige.
